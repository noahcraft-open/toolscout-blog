## What Is a Good Typing Speed?

| WPM Range | Level | Percentile |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| 0–20 | Beginner | Bottom 25% |
| 20–40 | Average | 25–50% |
| 40–60 | Above Average | 50–75% |
| 60–80 | Professional | 75–95% |
| 80–100 | Expert | Top 5% |
| 100+ | Elite | Top 1% |

The average typing speed is about 40 WPM. Professional typists and experienced developers typically type 60–80 WPM. Competitive speed typists can exceed 150 WPM.

## Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

### Touch Typing Fundamentals
Place your fingers on the home row: left hand on A-S-D-F, right hand on J-K-L-;. Your index fingers rest on F and J (the keys with bumps). Every other key is reached from this position.

### Focus on Accuracy First
Speed follows accuracy. If you consistently type at 95%+ accuracy, your speed will naturally increase. Typing fast with many errors trains bad habits.

### Daily Practice
Even 10 minutes of focused practice per day leads to measurable improvement within a week. Consistency beats intensity.

### Don't Look at the Keyboard
This is the hardest habit to break but the most important. Cover your keyboard with a cloth if necessary. Your muscle memory develops faster without visual feedback.
