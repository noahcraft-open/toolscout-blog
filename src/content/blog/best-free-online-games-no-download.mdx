**URL**: reaction-time-test-dun.vercel.app

- Color-coded rankings (Superhuman < 150ms to Slow > 400ms)
- Full-screen immersive experience
- Best/Average/Worst breakdown

**Best for**: Quick 30-second brain break, competitive fun with friends.
