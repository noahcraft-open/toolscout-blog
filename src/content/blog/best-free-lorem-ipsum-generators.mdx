## What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text derived from Cicero's *De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum* (45 BC), scrambled to be unrecognizable as classical Latin. It has been used in typesetting since the 1500s — long before the web existed.

The standard Lorem Ipsum passage begins:
> *Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.*

The text became widely used in web design in the 1980s when Aldus PageMaker began including it as a template.

### Why Lorem Ipsum Instead of Real Text?

Readable placeholder text distracts reviewers with content instead of design. If you use "The quick brown fox" or actual copy in a wireframe, stakeholders will read and edit the words instead of evaluating the layout. Lorem Ipsum sidesteps this entirely.

### The "Lorem Ipsum in Production" Bug

A common embarrassing bug: Lorem Ipsum placeholder text makes it to production. Common causes:
- Copy-paste from a design mockup
- Default template text in a CMS
- Test data not cleaned up before launch

**Prevention**: Search your codebase for "lorem ipsum" before deploying. Add it to your pre-deploy checklist.

## Lorem Ipsum and SEO

**Important**: Never publish Lorem Ipsum on a live website. Search engines index it as thin content and may penalize your site. Always replace placeholder text with real, valuable content before publishing.

For SEO purposes, even if a page isn't indexed yet, lorem ipsum text signals that the page is unfinished. Use `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` on any pages with placeholder content.

## Generating Lorem Ipsum for Specific Frameworks

### For Email Templates (MJML/HTML Email)

Email templates need to account for preview text length. Use about 90 characters of Lorem Ipsum to test how the preview text truncates in email clients.

### For Mobile Apps

Mobile screens have less space. Use 1–2 sentences per placeholder block, not full paragraphs. Test both short and long text cases — apps break in different ways when text exceeds its container.

### For Internationalization (i18n)

For testing translated content, use "long" dummy text. German text is typically 30% longer than English. Use Lorem Ipsum generators that let you specify word count, and test with 130% of your expected English length.
